Architecture

Studio Website

Where space becomes intention.

The Challenge

Portfolio site for a boutique architecture studio. Work speaks. Site should not interrupt. The design needed to reflect the studio's philosophy of minimalist intervention and profound impact, allowing their projects to narrate the story.

Client

NA Studio

Location

Paris

The Approach

Grid-based editorial layout. Full-bleed project images. Restraint as design language. The site was built to be a silent curator, guiding visitors through a visual narrative where each project unfolds with intentional pacing, mirroring architectural precision. Negative space was used as a structural element, not mere emptiness.

Deliverables

  • Website
  • Portfolio system
  • Press kit

The Result

Featured in Dezeen. Three new studio commissions within two months. The online presence solidified NA Studio's position in the high-end market, attracting clientele who value thoughtful design and a clear, unadulterated presentation of work. The site itself became a testament to their aesthetic.
