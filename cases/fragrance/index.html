Luxury Product

Fragrance Brand

The Mandate

Launch site for a conceptual fragrance brand. Product does not advertise. It implies.

Client

Eau Nulle

Location

Paris

Focus

Conceptual Reveal

The Method

Full-screen product reveal. Single scroll. No price. No cart. Inquiry only.

The site is an exercise in restraint. We created a single, continuous scroll experience that reveals the product story without conventional sales architecture. There is no navigation, no visible price, and no "add to cart" button. The entire journey is designed to build mystique, culminating in a simple inquiry form. It is a digital space that suggests value rather than stating it.

  • Website
  • E-commerce setup
  • Email flows

The Result

Sold out the first run of 200 units in 72 hours. Stockist in Paris and Dubai.
