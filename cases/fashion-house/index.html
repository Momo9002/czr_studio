Maison Identity

The collection lives here.

The Challenge

A new maison needing digital presence before its first runway. Identity established here first. The online platform was critical for articulating the brand's aesthetic and philosophy, setting the stage for its debut collection and distinguishing it in a competitive landscape.

Client

Elara

Location

Milan

The Approach

Editorial lookbook. Collection pages. Press access. No e-commerce — appointment only. The site was crafted as an exclusive digital atelier, prioritizing visual storytelling and curated access over transactional functions, embodying the brand's commitment to bespoke luxury and personal engagement.

Deliverables

  • Website
  • Lookbook system
  • Press portal

The Result

Picked up by two buyers at Pitti Uomo. Press credentials issued. The digital identity successfully positioned Elara as a serious contender in haute couture, attracting critical attention and securing vital industry connections even before its physical collection was fully unveiled. A foundation for future growth.
