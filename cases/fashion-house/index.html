Fashion

Maison Identity

The Challenge

The identity is established here first.

A new maison needs a digital presence before its first runway. This is not a portfolio of past work; it is the debut collection itself. The website must embody the brand's entire philosophy, serving as a confident statement of intent to press, buyers, and future clients. It must feel established from day one.

Client: Elara
Location: Milan

The Build

The collection lives here.

We built an editorial lookbook, not a product grid. Collection pages are designed as chapters in a story. To maintain exclusivity, there is no e-commerce. Viewings are by appointment only. A secure portal grants press access to high-resolution assets and official statements, controlling the narrative from the very beginning.

  • Website
  • Lookbook system
  • Press portal

The Result

Picked up by two buyers at Pitti Uomo.

The pre-runway digital presence generated significant industry interest. The lookbook was strong enough to secure appointments, resulting in two buyer commitments at the prestigious Pitti Uomo trade show. Press credentials were issued to major publications, cementing the maison's legitimacy before a single piece walked the runway.
