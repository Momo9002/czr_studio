Fashion

Maison Identity

The Mandate

A new maison needing digital presence before its first runway. Identity established here first.

Client

Elara

Location

Milan

Focus

Digital First

The Method

Editorial lookbook. Collection pages. Press access. No e-commerce — appointment only.

The website was designed to serve as the brand's first editorial statement. We created a digital lookbook with full-screen, high-resolution imagery and minimal text, allowing the collection to define the identity. Access is curated; there is no public-facing store. Instead, a discreet portal allows press and buyers to request appointments, establishing the brand's exclusivity from day one.

  • Website
  • Lookbook system
  • Press portal

The Result

Picked up by two buyers at Pitti Uomo. Press credentials issued.
