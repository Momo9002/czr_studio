Hospitality

Restaurant Flagship

The Mandate

A private dining experience with no online presence. Reservations by referral only. The site had to feel like the evening itself.

Client

TABLE N°1

Location

Monaco

Focus

Brand Immersion

The Method

Full-bleed chapter-based scroll. No conventional nav. Each section a scene.

The digital experience was crafted to mirror the intimacy of the physical space. We eliminated traditional navigation in favor of a narrative-driven scroll, where each viewport acts as a chapter in a story. The design language uses deep, warm tones from the client's palette to evoke the feeling of a candlelit dinner, focusing on atmosphere over information.

  • Website
  • Brand identity
  • Photography direction

The Result

Reservation requests tripled in the first week. No price listed. No question asked.
