Director Portfolio

The Challenge

Direction without apology.

How do you unify a body of work that spans fashion, film, and music? The portfolio for a director like Baron D. couldn't be a simple gallery. It needed to be an archive, a statement, a digital gallery that presented diverse projects with a single, confident voice. The site itself had to have a strong point of view.

Client: Baron D.
Location: NYC

The Build

Site as Gallery.

We designed a grid-based archive that allows for intuitive exploration of a vast body of work. Case studies are presented full-screen, with video-first sections to immediately immerse the visitor. The user experience is akin to walking through a gallery, moving from one major work to the next, each presented with maximum impact.

  • Video showcase
  • Contact system
