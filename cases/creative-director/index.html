The Method

Grid-based archive. Full-screen case studies. Video-first sections.

The architecture is a clean, structural grid that functions as a digital gallery. Each project in the archive opens into an immersive, full-screen case study where video and large-format photography lead. The interface is deliberately understated to ensure the director's work is the sole focus. It's a platform built for showcasing, not distraction.
