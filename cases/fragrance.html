How We Work
72 Bottles. No Restock.

Each edition is produced once. 72 bottles — no more, no reprints. When it's gone, it becomes part of a collector's history.

Single Key Ingredient.

Every fragrance is built around one raw material of exceptional quality. Everything else serves that ingredient, not the other way around.

Hand-blown Bottles.

Each bottle is individually mouth-blown by a glassmaker in Murano. No two are identical. The vessel is part of the work.
