---

## Visual Style Rules

- **Background:** Always pure black `#000000`
- **Headlines:** Syne 800, white, large, bold
- **Body text:** Manrope 400, `#999` or white depending on hierarchy
- **Color accents:** Mondrian rays as atmospheric light effects (not flat fills)
- **Device mockups:** Silver/dark laptop frames with portfolio screenshots
- **Watermark:** `CZR.STUDIO` or `czr.studio` bottom-left or bottom-right
