# CZR Studio — Social Media Guidelines

> Platform-specific rules for content creation and posting.

---

## Global Rules

1. **No AI mention** — ever. Not in captions, comments, DMs, or stories.
2. **Quality over quantity** — 2-3 posts/week beats daily mediocre content.
3. **Visual consistency** — every post should look like it belongs to the same brand.
4. **Dark aesthetic** — black backgrounds, white type, Mondrian accents.

---

## Instagram (@czr.studio)

### Posting Cadence
- **2× per week** minimum (Tue/Thu or Mon/Wed)
- **Stories:** 2-3× per week for engagement
- **Best posting times:** 10:00 CET (morning EU) or 18:00 GST (evening Dubai)

### Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Portfolio showcase | 1×/week | Show work quality, attract clients |
| Process / behind-the-scenes | 1×/2 weeks | Build trust, show craft |
| Founder story | 1×/month | Personal connection, authority |
| Text/quote post | 1×/2 weeks | Engagement, shareability |
| Client result | When available | Social proof, conversion |

### Caption Template

```
[Hook — bold statement, max 10 words]

[Context — 2 sentences, what + result]

[CTA — "Link in bio" or "DM for founding rate"]

#CZRStudio #HauteCoutureDigital #WebDesign [+3-5 relevant tags]
```

### Image Specs

| Format | Dimensions | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Feed post (square) | 1080 × 1080 px | Default |
| Feed post (portrait) | 1080 × 1350 px | Portfolio showcases |
| Story | 1080 × 1920 px | Stories, announcements |
| Carousel | 1080 × 1080 px per slide | Process reels, multi-image |

### Story Style

- Black background, white Syne headlines
- Red accent bars or dots
- Swipe-up CTA in Manrope uppercase
- Poll/question stickers: white text on dark surface

## LinkedIn

### Posting Cadence
- **1× per week** (best: Tuesday or Wednesday morning)

### Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Founder insight / story | 2×/month | Authority, personal brand |
| Case study breakdown | 1×/month | Demonstrate expertise |
| Industry observation | 1×/month | Thought leadership |

### Post Template

```
[Opening hook — personal, provocative, or contrarian]

[Story or observation — 3-5 short paragraphs]

[Insight or lesson — what this means for the reader]

[Soft CTA — "What do you think?" or link to site]
```

### Rules

- Write in first person (founder voice)
- No hashtags in body text — max 3 at the end if any
- No emojis in text body
- Keep paragraphs to 1-2 sentences for mobile readability
- Never use "I'm excited to announce" or similar clichés

---

## WhatsApp (Outreach / DMs)

### Opening Message Template

```
Hi [Name] — saw your [brand/studio/work] and really liked [specific thing].

Quick question: are you happy with your current site, or is an upgrade on the radar?

Either way, great work. Cheers, Amaury / CZR Studio
```

### Rules

- Always compliment something specific
- Never send a sales pitch in the first message
- Keep it to 3 lines max
- Follow up once after 48h if no reply — then stop
- Never automate DMs (personal touch only)
