# CZR Studio — Brand Usage Rules

> Do's and don'ts for applying the brand across any medium.

---

## Logo Usage

### ✅ Do

- Always use `CZR.` with the trailing period
- Always set in **Syne 800** (or closest bold geometric sans)
- Maintain clear space of at least `1em` around the wordmark
- Use white on dark backgrounds, black on light backgrounds
- Animate the red underline on hover (web only)

### ❌ Don't

- Never use lowercase (`czr.` or `Czr.`)
- Never stretch, rotate, or distort the wordmark
- Never add drop shadows or effects to the logo
- Never place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Never mix the logo with other typefaces in the same lockup
- Never remove the trailing period

---

## Color Usage

### Primary Palette Application

- **Background is always black** (`#000000`) — no dark gray, no off-black
- **White is the primary UI color** — buttons, headings, interactive elements
- **Mondrian colors are accents only** — never as fills for large areas
  - Red `#C8242A` → progress bars, first accents, underlines
  - Yellow `#F0C029` → secondary accents, tile highlights
  - Blue `#1A4BA8` → tertiary accents, alternate tiles

### Rules

- Maximum 2 Mondrian colors visible at once in any section
- Never use gradients between Mondrian colors (they don't blend)
- Mondrian tiles in the portfolio grid are the exception — they can be solid fills
- All color appears against black — never on white or gray backgrounds

---

## Typography Rules

- Headlines: **Syne 800**, tracking `-0.04em`, line-height `1.0`
- Body: **Manrope 400**, color `#999999`, line-height `1.65`
- Eyebrows: **Manrope 700**, uppercase, tracking `0.22em`, color `#555555`
- Buttons: **Manrope 700**, uppercase, tracking `0.08em`
- Never mix in other typefaces
- Never use Syne below 700 weight for display text

---

## Image Treatment

- Portfolio images: **grayscale 30%** + **opacity 65%** at rest
- Hover: remove grayscale, reduce opacity to 45%, zoom `1.06×`
- Always use AI-generated or stock imagery (no client photos without permission)
- Style: high-end, editorial, photography-grade quality
- Never use illustrations, clip art, or cartoon-style imagery

---

## Corners & Shapes

- **All corners are sharp** — `border-radius: 0` everywhere
- No rounded buttons, no pill shapes, no circles for avatars (use squares)
- Grid lines use `1px` at `rgba(255,255,255,0.10)`

---

## Social Media Assets

### Profile Picture

- Solid black square with `CZR.` wordmark centered in white
- No gradient, no icon, no image — wordmark only

### Instagram Grid

- Maintain visual consistency: dark backgrounds, high-contrast type
- Alternate between portfolio showcases and text-based posts
- Every image should feel like it belongs in a luxury magazine

### Story Templates

- Black background, white text, red accent elements
- Syne for headlines, Manrope for body copy
- Swipe-up CTAs in uppercase with arrow

---

## Co-Branding

- CZR logo always appears first (left or top) in co-branded materials
- Minimum separation between logos: `2em`
- Never combine the CZR wordmark with another brand's logo in a single lockup
- Partner logos should be in monochrome (white or gray) to match our palette
