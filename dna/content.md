# CZR Studio — Content DNA

> Read by: Instagram scheduling, campaign scripts, portfolio system

---

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Ratio | Mode | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Portfolio — Light** | 35% | Cream | Device on linen/cream desk, natural light |
| **Portfolio — Dark** | 15% | Dark | Device on marble, cinematic lighting |
| **The Knight** | 20% | Dark | Avatar in editorial product scenes |
| **Process Carousel** | 15% | Alternating | 5 slides: brief → proof |
| **Axiom** | 15% | Alternating | Single philosophy sentence on cream or black |

---

## Grid Pattern (Instagram 3-column)

```
[Dark: Knight]      [Light: Portfolio]  [Dark: Portfolio]
[Light: Portfolio]  [Dark: Axiom]       [Light: Portfolio]
[Dark: Knight]      [Light: Carousel]   [Dark: Portfolio]
```

**Rules:**
- Knight appears every 6 posts (1 per every 2 rows)
- Never 3 dark posts in a row
- Never 3 light posts in a row

## Reels Strategy (no face)

### Priority order (highest to lowest impact)

1. **Screen recording of finished site** — scroll through client site, cinematic audio
2. **Before/after** — split screen old vs CZR-built site
3. **Knight reveal** — slow camera push to Knight on desk, laptop reveals site in bg
4. **Design timelapse** — Figma/code appearing at 8x speed, trending audio
5. **Text reveal** — pure black, words appear one at a time, light flash at end

**Audio:** Always instrumental. No lyrics. Ryuichi Sakamoto, Nils Frahm, Hans Zimmer piano. Or trending lo-fi/ambient.

**Never:** text-on-black slideshows compiled into video.

## Story Templates

- Background: `#000000`
- Knight icon: top-left, 32px
- Footer: thin red line + "CZR STUDIO" centered
- Content: work-in-progress screenshots, before/afters, poll ("A or B?")
- Frequency: 1-2/day max

## Post Deletion Criteria

Remove a post if:
- It has 0 engagement after 14 days
- It breaks the visual rhythm (wrong mode, inconsistent style)
- It was created before the Knight DNA was adopted
- It contains text-on-black graphics

---

## Campaign ↔ Content Sync Rules

- The active Meta ad image must match the current Instagram visual mode (if posting light week, run light ad)
- Caption voice must be identical: same templates, same sign-off
- Every ad links to czr.studio — not a landing page
