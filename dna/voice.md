# CZR Studio — Voice DNA

> Read by: agents, campaign scripts, copywriters, social posts

---

## The Voice Model

**Hermès copywriter × SpaceX mission brief.**

Hermès: quiet confidence, never explains, lets silence do the work.
SpaceX: precision, mission-critical, no decoration.

Together: luxury that means business.

---

## Core Rules

1. **Never sell. State.** — "We deliver in 48 hours." Not "We can deliver in 48 hours!"
2. **Period endings.** Every sentence. No exclamation marks. Ever.
3. **Short sentences.** One idea per sentence. Maximum 15 words.
4. **Never explain. Imply.** — "From brief to live." Not "Our process takes you from the initial briefing call all the way to a live website."
5. **"We do" not "we can."** Ability is assumed. Action is stated.
6. **No emojis.** In copy, captions, or agent messages.
7. **No buzzwords.** Banned: amazing, excited, leverage, synergy, innovative, cutting-edge, game-changing, disruptive.

---

## Banned Words

```
amazing, excited, leverage, synergy, innovative, cutting-edge,
game-changing, disruptive, seamless, holistic, bespoke (use "made"),
solution (use "site" or "work"), deliverable, onboarding (use "start")
```

## Tone by Context

| Context | Adjust how |
|---|---|
| Instagram captions | Most minimal — let image carry |
| WhatsApp (agents) | Warm, precise, conversational — but never casual |
| Website body copy | Clinical, confident, no decoration |
| Ad copy | One punchy line + CTA |
| Portfolio captions | Factual: project + result + time |
| Error states | Direct: "Something went wrong. DM us." |

---

## The CZR Sign-off

Always one of:
- `→ czr.studio`
- `DM to start.`
- `Brief us.`

Never: "Contact us today!", "Click the link in bio!", "Drop a comment below!"
